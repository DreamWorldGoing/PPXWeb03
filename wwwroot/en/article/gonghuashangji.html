Beibu Gulf Bank of Guangxi and overseas Chinese businessmen talk about business opportunities

来源：广西新闻网 2011年10月27日 10:21

On the evening of October 23, Guangxi Beibu Gulf Bank held a "Guangxi Friendship Wine with Overseas Chinese Businessmen" to invite overseas Chinese businessmen to participate in the 8th China-ASEAN Expo and the 8th China-ASEAN Business and Investment Summit to exchange and dialogue with government officials and entrepreneurs of the autonomous region to seek business opportunities for cooperation.

Dozens of overseas Chinese businessmen from Britain, Canada, Japan, the United States, the Netherlands, Hungary and other countries and regions, during the Expo they have visited Chongzuo, Nanning, Qinzhou and other major cities in Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone. At the Friendship Meeting, the relevant departments of the autonomous region introduced the investment environment, preferential policies and future development plans for the construction of Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone in recent years. They hoped that overseas Chinese, Chinese businessmen and overseas students would return to their hometown to start their own businesses and actively participate in the development of key industries such as electronics, information, biology, medicine and new materials in Guangxi so as to contribute to the economy of Guangxi. We should introduce more and better projects and talents to the economic and social development.

As an important financial force in Guangxi, by the end of September, Guangxi Beibu Gulf Bank had realized a total assets of 84.571 billion yuan, 10 times as much as before the reform and restructuring, and successfully stepped into the ranks of medium-sized city commercial banks in China.
