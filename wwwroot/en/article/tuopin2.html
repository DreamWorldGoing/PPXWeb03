Helping the fight against poverty and strengthening the battle - increasing people's sense of gain

来源：工业交通处 |广西财政厅 2017-07-04

Rural transportation infrastructure construction plays a supporting role in poverty alleviation and poverty alleviation. It is the first move to achieve accurate poverty alleviation and accurate poverty alleviation. It is a key measure to solve the bottleneck of economic and social development in poverty-stricken areas. Guangxi Finance has vigorously supported the rural traffic alleviation and poverty alleviation as an important measure to thoroughly study and implement General Secretary Xi Jinping's inspection of the spirit of Guangxi's important speech, and accelerate the construction of rural “outside inland, Tongcun Changxiang, shuttle to village, safe and convenient” transportation. The network will improve the level of equalization of basic public services for transportation, continuously increase the “sense of gain” of the people, and improve the ability of poverty-stricken areas to get rid of poverty.

First, multi-pronged measures, the autonomous region's finances strongly support the construction of rural transportation infrastructure

(1) Strongly promote and expand the scope of support for poverty alleviation. (2) The government leads and raises the subsidy standards for transportation poverty alleviation. (3) Accurate policy, focusing on poverty-stricken areas. (4) Deepen reform and broaden financing channels for poverty alleviation through transportation. (5) Improve the mechanism and strengthen the maintenance and management of rural transportation infrastructure. (6) Advance deployment, speeding up the pace of poverty alleviation through poverty alleviation.

Second, long-term development, Guangxi's traffic has been effective in poverty alleviation

(1) Stable and steady, and the achievements in rural transportation infrastructure construction are remarkable.                  As of the end of 2016, the total mileage of Guangxi Highway exceeded 120,000 kilometers, and the highway density reached 50.69 kilometers/100 square kilometers. (2) In an orderly manner, the capacity of rural transportation services has been greatly improved.                  The transportation network is more perfect, and large-scale hub stations such as Baise Chengdong Passenger Station are built. Basically, all county-level passenger stations are built with more than two levels, and 667 rural passenger stations with three levels and below are built, and 7146 convenience bus shelters are built.
