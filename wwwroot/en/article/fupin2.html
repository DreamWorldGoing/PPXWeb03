Since the reform and opening up - China's rural poor population has decreased by 740 million people

来源：人民日报 | 作者：陈炜伟 | 2018-09-04 09:20:11

According to a report released by the National Bureau of Statistics on the 3rd, from 1978 to 2017, the number of rural poor in China decreased by 740 million, and the annual average poverty reduction population was close to 19 million. The incidence of rural poverty fell by 94.4 percentage points, with an average annual decline of 2.4. Percentage points.

Statistics show that over the past 40 years of reform and opening up, through deepening reforms and large-scale poverty alleviation and development, China has significantly reduced the number of poor people and contributed more than 70% to global poverty reduction.              In addition, the income of rural residents in poverty-stricken areas has maintained rapid growth. The data show that in 2017, the per capita disposable income of rural residents in poverty-stricken areas was 9,377 yuan. After deducting the price factor, the actual level was 1.6 times that of 2012, and the average annual real growth rate was 10.4%, which was 2.5 percentage points faster than the national average rural growth rate. In 2017, the per capita disposable income of rural residents in poverty-stricken areas was 69.8% of the national average in rural areas, an increase of 7.7 percentage points over 2012.
