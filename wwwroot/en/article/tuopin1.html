Implementing the important measures for poverty alleviation

来源：广西扶贫信息网 | 作者：龙博 2018-07-04 11:18:32

On July 3, the Leading Group for Poverty Alleviation and Development of the Autonomous Region conducted an interview on the party and government leaders of 17 counties (cities, districts) with the results of the poverty alleviation and development in 2017. Sun Dawei, deputy secretary of the Party Committee of the autonomous region and deputy head of the Leading Group for Poverty Alleviation and Development of the Autonomous Region, attended and spoke. The meeting was presided over by Fang Chunming, vice chairman of the autonomous region. Jiang Jiabai, deputy secretary general of the autonomous regional government and director of the poverty alleviation office, attended the meeting.

The meeting separately interviewed three poverty-stricken counties in Sanjiang Dong Autonomous County, Napo County, Yucheng County, Jinxiu Yao Autonomous County, Fuchuan Yao Autonomous County, Longyu District, Tengzhou City, Tianzhu County and Guanyang County. There are 8 non-poor counties in Guilin Lingui District, Xing'an County, Qinzhou Qinnan District, Laibin City Xingbin District, Lingshan County, Nanning Liangqing District, Liuzhou Liubei District and Dongxing City. The meeting detailed the outstanding problems in the counties (cities, districts) that were interviewed, and made a profound review speech in the order of the party and government leaders in the interview.

Sun Dawei emphasized that this interview is an important measure to implement the internship of General Secretary Jin Ping’s spirit of assessing and assessing poverty alleviation. It is a concrete action to tighten the responsibility of strengthening and resolving poverty and improve the quality of poverty alleviation. It is necessary to comprehensively and profoundly understand this interview, turn pressure into motivation, and quickly reverse the passive situation of poverty alleviation. In response to the problems raised by the state and the autonomous regions, we must take the initiative to identify and refrain from doing the same thing. We must resolutely prevent the problems of formalism, bureaucracy, and falsification in the work of getting rid of poverty, and do a good job in all aspects with a good work style. It is necessary to take poverty alleviation as the top priority and the first livelihood project, and do a solid job in industrial poverty alleviation, poverty alleviation, education poverty alleviation, and medical poverty alleviation, and resolutely win the battle against poverty. The responsible persons of the Organizational Department of the Party Committee of the Autonomous Region, the Poverty Alleviation Office of the Autonomous Region, and the Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee, the Minister of the Organization Department, the Deputy Mayor in charge of poverty alleviation, and the Director of the Poverty Alleviation Office attended the meeting.
