Multi-national city emergency rescue experts gathered in Nanning to improve the ability of disaster relief

记者 叶昊鸣、潘强 2018-07-09 20:55:40 来源： 新华网

On the 9th, nearly 600 people from 12 ASEAN regional forum members and 4 international organizations from China, Malaysia and Vietnam gathered in Nanning, Guangxi, and held a four-day seminar on related topics such as “Urban Emergency Rescue”.

Wei Weidong, deputy director of the Fire Department of the China Emergency Management Department, said in his opening speech that the Chinese emergency management department attaches great importance to exchanges and cooperation with the emergency rescue departments of the world, and learns from the advanced concepts and useful practices of urban emergency management in various countries around the world. The second ASEAN Regional Forum Urban Emergency Rescue Seminar will be held in Nanning to enhance communication and understanding and exchanges and cooperation in the field of emergency rescue. It is conducive to reaching more consensus and improving the ability and level of all parties to jointly resist disaster accidents.

Zain, deputy director of the Malaysian National Disaster Management Bureau, said that the development of urbanization, climate change, geological, hydrological and man-made hazards have made disasters more frequent and have increased the impact of disasters. The seminar will be a platform for exchange and cooperation training and knowledge sharing, so that all parties can be prepared to deal with regional disasters in the event of natural disasters.

In the academic exchanges of the seminar, rescue experts from China, the United States, Australia, Singapore, Vietnam and other countries will give keynote speeches on disaster prevention and control and disposal technologies in key industries, and rescue teams from relevant countries and regions and China. The firefighting and rescue team of the fire brigade will conduct on-site technical exchanges and competitions, and participate in large-scale petrochemical fire fighting and rescue cross-regional emergency drills in Qinzhou, Guangxi.

The ASEAN Regional Forum Urban Emergency Rescue Seminar is a seminar organized by the Chinese side under the framework of the ASEAN Regional Forum and with the theme of urban emergency rescue. The first ASEAN Regional Forum Urban Emergency Rescue Seminar was held in Shanghai in 2016.
