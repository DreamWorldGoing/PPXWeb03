Miu Jianhua: a new chapter on medicinal plants

Author: Wu Shiqi   source: "contemporary Guangxi" 2018 issue sixteenth. 2018-08-22 15:57:15

He led the pharmacy out of its predicament to become the world's largest medicinal botanical garden certified by the Guinness headquarters. He is committed to developing cooperation with ASEAN countries, so that Guangxi's excellent traditional Chinese medicine resources and planting technology "go out". In Nanning, there is a quiet place where Maolin drips green, rattan blossoms entangled, and the flow of water is murmuring. It is known as the Guangxi Medicinal Botanical Garden with the three-dimensional "Compendium of Materia Medica". In 2011, it was certified as the world's "largest medicinal botanical garden" by the UK Guinness headquarters to conserve the largest variety of medicinal plants and cultivate the largest area of medicinal plants. This achievement is inseparable from the founding of this brilliant first hero - Miao Jianhua.

"Chinese medicine is a beautiful encounter between heaven, earth and human beings!" This is Miu Jianhua's description of medicinal plants through poetic language. When Miao Jianhua came to Guangxi Medicinal Botanical Garden in early 2001, it was still a unit that had difficulty in surviving. Seeing this situation, he began to concentrate on the study of Chinese medicine resources, medicinal plant ecology, determined to become an expert in the field of medicinal resources, leading the pharmacy out of trouble.

Miao Jianhua has been expanding his knowledge, transforming himself from a layman to an expert, and leading his team to create the subject of "Medicinal Plant Conservation", which was listed as a key subject of Chinese medicine by the State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 2013. He also creatively put forward the management system of traditional Chinese medicine resources, which provides a good decision-making reference for promoting the protection, development and sustainable utilization of traditional Chinese medicine resources.

In order to improve the research level and industrialization development level of Guangxi traditional Chinese medicine, Miao Jianhua planned and organized the National Development and Reform Commission's "Guangxi large-scale genuine and endangered rare medicinal materials GAP breeding base" and other major national projects, and at the same time constructed the "national essential drugs and major disease raw materials base construction project in Guangxi", for a wide range of projects. Western China has introduced a number of high-quality personnel, built a good research platform, and achieved a number of high-level scientific research results, promoting the development of Guangxi's cause of traditional Chinese medicine.

In order to promote the development of the Chinese medicine industry and promote the deep development of the medicinal resources along the belt, Miu Jianhua jointly launched the "4 plan of medicinal plants" jointly with the Shenzhen national gene bank, China University of genetics, Macao University and Beijing computing center in 2017. Miu Jianhua has been committed to working with ASEAN countries. He said that the pharmacy will continue to seek cooperation with Singapore, Malaysia and other countries, ASEAN countries will be excellent drug resources and technology "to bring in", let the region's excellent Chinese medicine resources and planting technology "out".
