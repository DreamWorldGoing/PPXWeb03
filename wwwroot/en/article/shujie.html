Kleenex: "the most beautiful Chinese road sister"

Author: Wang Zhenzheng rejuvenating &nsbp Source: "contemporary Guangxi" 2016 twenty-third issue 2016-12-05 11:31:04

Heart service promotion industry benchmark

The Guixiang toll station at the junction of Guangxi and Hunan expressway, known as the "North Gate of Guangxi", is an important gateway for Guangxi to enter the Sanxiang land and the hinterland of the Central Plains, and an important window to show the "image of Guangxi". In this daily South-to-North traffic lane, Guixiang toll station staff a warm and gentle smile, as well as their silent quality of service for vehicles, won the unanimous recognition of the public. Recently, Guixiang toll station toll collector Shu Jie was awarded the title of "the most beautiful Chinese road sister" in the third term, and was the only advanced individual in Guangxi who won the honor in 2016.

In recent years, the Party Committee and the government of the autonomous region have vigorously implemented the strategy of giving priority to the development of transportation and speeded up the construction of expressways in Guangxi. With the completion of the main skeleton of Guangxi expressway network, the responsibility of window service industry is increasing. "Guixiang Toll Station is an inter-provincial toll station at the junction of Guangxi and Hunan. It has a large volume of freight cars, a high toll collection amount, a heavy task of safety and smoothness, and a higher and higher demand for toll collection speed and service quality for toll collectors." GUI Xiang charging station owner Zhang Zhiming introduced.

Shu Jie has been a toll collector in Guixiang toll station since 2011. He has been smiling and greeting 600 times a day, turning around and nodding more than 1200 times a day. Every time, she was meticulous and smiling all the time. "Simple things repeat, do things repeatedly" is the working principle of Kleenex. In order to ensure the safe and rapid passage of vehicles at toll stations, she took the initiative to study ways to improve the efficiency of toll collection, and summarized how to avoid toll errors. By July 2016, she had achieved a record of continuous toll collection of 70 million yuan without errors, which became the benchmark of Guangxi highway industry.

"In order to improve the speed of money counting, she often practices in the dormitory with coupons, earnestly learning the anti-counterfeiting knowledge of coins." Her colleagues in the same group praised her. Shu Jie also shared his experience with colleagues, bringing out a 50 million yuan error-free expert Yang Shun and a person who fled a single amount of up to 350,000 yuan Wei Zhiyao and so on.

Leaking levied to safeguard the interests of state-owned enterprises

Guixiang toll station is located on the expressway trunk road at the provincial boundary. It has large traffic flow, complex charging environment and many phenomena of evading tolls. After the behavior of evading fees has been detected, drivers abuse the toll collectors, maliciously block the lanes and other disturbances to the normal charging order have occurred from time to time. "On a cold and rainy winter night, a driver of a fake live car refused to cooperate with the inspection. He not only spoke foul language, but also shoved me to the ground." Shu Jie said that when her hands were red with blood instantly, she endured pain and patiently explained the charging policy. Finally, she moved the truck driver. She not only apologized voluntarily, paid the toll in full, but also ensured that she would never fake a live car again.

"Another time, a fake live car was seized, and the driver had to pay the toll in full. The driver was instantly angry, not only scolding, but spitting on the RMB thrown over." Shu Jie said that in this case, we should not only explain the charging policy patiently, but also resolve the contradictions with a smile to ensure the safety and unimpeded charging order. "Shujie has integrity and a strong sense of responsibility. Every day something like this happens that dares to fight against illegal evasion of funds." GUI Xiang charging station Zhang Zhiming said. It is "Shu Jie" with practical action, to avoid the loss of state property, truly achieved the grain warehouse, trickle back to justice.

Smiling service shows Guangxi's image

Guangxi Expressway window service industry has developed rapidly, the number of employees has increased rapidly, the industry standards have gradually taken shape, and the service quality has been continuously improved. Guixiang toll station toll collectors adhere to the "smiling with warm companion" fee service management standards, adhere to the work position, and make every effort to ensure the safety and unimpeded freeway.

In the spring of 2015, there was not enough motorists at the toll stations. Shu Jie, who suffered from a bad cold and was resting in his dormitory, did not say anything about "going to the island" support. A Hubei driver saw her coughing and commanding the vehicle in the cold wind. He handed him a bottle of cough syrup. Shu Jie smiled and waved his hand and said, "Thank you, Master. No, you're the best help for me to pass quickly according to my command." Near the Spring Festival in 2016, Guixiang toll station suffered from the "overlord" cold wave, the temperature dropped to below zero degrees Celsius, ice resistance and smooth work is extremely serious. At that time, Kleenex was told that her mother had been in a hospital when she was in a car accident.

"On the one hand is the arduous task of fluency, on the other hand is the safety of the mother, although I would like to grow wings to fly to the mother, but I can not leave, because I hope that more children can return to their parents safely!" Kleenex resolutely joined the colleagues in the first line of Bao Chang. The civilized service of inter-provincial toll stations shows the image of Guangxi. Shu Jie shows the image and elegant demeanor of Guangxi expressway service industry in the new period with good image and quality service to ensure the safety and smooth flow of expressways.
