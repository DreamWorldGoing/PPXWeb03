Experts and scholars gathered to talk about the rule of law in Ethnic Education

来源：广西新闻网-广西日报 2017年12月11日 06:41

Media experts and scholars from government organs at all levels, ethnic research institutions, colleges and universities, practical circles as well as media experts and scholars who mainly publicize ethnic content gathered to talk about the legalization of ethnic education development in Guangxi, to further sort out the key contents of ethnic education and to promote legislation, and to call for the early establishment of ethnic education with Guangxi ethnic characteristics. Laws and regulations.

The draft proposal was drafted by the Guangxi Education Science Special Project Investigation and Research on the Construction of Rule of Law in Guangxi Ethnic Education under the leadership of the Education Department of the Autonomous Region. This seminar mainly focuses on the legitimacy, necessity and feasibility of the bill, listening to the views of all parties, reflecting the requirements of open-door legislation.

The participants reached a consensus that strengthening and perfecting the legislation of ethnic education is an urgent task for the development of ethnic education in our region, and the legislation of ethnic education is imperative. It is reported that this regulation will be reported to the autonomous region government as part of the 2018 legislative plan, striving to promulgate this regulation before the 60th anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous region.
