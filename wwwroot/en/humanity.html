Dance poem "Tong"

Director Zhang Jigang has taken root in Guangxi for 20 years and has performed three outstanding plays in the Jinjing show, "White Lotus", "Bagui Big Song" and "Tong", and once again came to Beijing to perform. If there is a "Xanadu" in the world, then you must take a look at the dance poem "Tong". Liuzhou Art Theatre invited the domestic first-class creative team. After six years of hard work, it launched a natural, simple, dreamy and happy "Tong".

National Song and Dance Drama "White Lotus"

It tells the story of Bailian, a young woman who loves to sing, eager for freedom and love, to save her father and her relatives, and finally to go to the sea of fire with her lover. Under the careful arrangement and creation of director Zhang Jigang, the 2017 edition of "White Lotus" has joined the modern aesthetic elements to show the audience the vitality of Liuzhou in the artistic innovation.

National Sound Painting "Bagui Big Song"

Taking "using the people's language to shape the image of the people and praising the people's life with the songs of the people" is the pursuit of art. The original ecological Guangxi folk songs and folk customs are used to string together the "labor articles" and "love articles". 40 simple and beautiful folk songs, praising the colorful life of various ethnic groups in Guangxi, is known as "the spring snow that is refined and sublimated from the real life of the lower Libyan people."

LiYun
Explorer of Guangxi Railway Soaring
Founder of Guangxi Railway's take-off

In 2018, the 40th anniversary of the reform and opening up, the 60th anniversary of the establishment of the autonomous region, 12 of the 14 districts and cities in Guangxi have been driven by the railway network, the railway network has realized "Beitong, Nanda, Dongjin, Xilian" from the "road network terminal" Developed as a “regional hub”. The convenient transportation network has driven the rapid development of the economy, making Guangxi a new node for the organic integration of the “Belt and Road” land and sea trade.

"Guangxi's local economy needs to develop greatly and develop rapidly. The railway must also develop greatly and develop rapidly." The development of Guangxi's economy is inseparable from the development of the railway, and the development of Guangxi Railway is inseparable. An old railway man who said this, his name is Li Yun.

Shujie
"The most beautiful Chinese road sister"

The Guixiang toll station at the junction of Guangxi and Hunan Expressway is known as the “Guangxi Gate” and is an important gateway for Guangxi to enter the Sanxiang land and the hinterland of the Central Plains. It has become an important window for the “Guangxi image”. On this passage that runs from south to north every day, the staff of Guixiang Toll Station have a gentle smile and a high-quality service that they silently provide for the vehicles, which has been unanimously recognized by the public. Recently, Gui Jie, the toll collector of Guixiang Toll Station, was awarded the title of “The Most Beautiful Chinese Road Sister” in the third year. It is the only advanced individual in Guangxi who won this award in 2016.

Yan Jianhua
A new chapter in the medicinal plant business

He led the drug garden out of the woods and became the world's "largest medicinal botanical garden" certified by the Guinness headquarters. He is committed to cooperating with ASEAN countries to “go out” the excellent Chinese medicine resources and planting techniques in Guangxi.                      In Nanning, there is a winding path of Maolin Dicui, vines wrapped around, and flowing water. It is the Guangxi Medicinal Botanical Garden, which is known as the "three-dimensional "Compendium of Materia Medica". In 2011, it was the world's "largest medicinal botanical garden" with the largest number of medicinal plant species and the largest area for planting medicinal plants. The achievement of this achievement is inseparable from the brilliant first. The hero - Yan Jianhua.
